Bytestruck 2015

Date: 04-11-2015

Venue: Edu Sat Hall

On 4'th of Nov 2015, Department of Computer Science and Engineering PACE, conducted BYTESTRUCK-2015, a showcase event to explore the open software usefulness to the High school and PU students. This event was organized for spreading awareness of Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) among students and hence encourage them to use FOSS.
